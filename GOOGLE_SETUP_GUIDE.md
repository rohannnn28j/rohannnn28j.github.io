## Step 1: Host Your Website (REQUIRED)

Your website is currently on your computer. Google can't find it. You need to upload it to the internet.

### Option A: FREE Hosting with GitHub Pages (RECOMMENDED)
1. Go to https://github.com
2. Create a free account
3. Create a new repository named `yourusername.github.io`
4. Upload all your files (index.html, styles.css, script.js, admin.html, admin-styles.css, admin-script.js, sitemap.xml, robots.txt)
5. Your website will be at: `https://yourusername.github.io`

**Time: 5-10 minutes | Cost: FREE**

## Step 3: Register with Google Search Console

1. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
2. Click "Start Now"
3. Add your website URL
4. Verify ownership (follow their instructions)
5. Submit sitemap.xml
6. Submit robots.txt

**This tells Google about your website!**

---

## Step 4: Register with Google Business Profile

1. Go to https://www.google.com/business/
2. Create a business profile
3. Add your name, contact details, services
4. Add your website URL

**This helps local SEO!**
